My Love for Fishing & Technology

I was always an outdoor child growing up. I would immerse myself in nature. I was always in the yard digging up worms or catching spiders. Eventually I got into fishing and my obsession with the outdoors really took off. I was riding my bike out of my Southside neighborhood to the suburbs to fish whenever I could. Eventually I got my drive's license and a car. With this came fishing at locations my bike couldn't reach. When I was 17 I took a Computer Science class. To my surprise I really liked it.

Similiar to fishing, coding was a lot of problem solving and I loved that. It was a new challenge to me and it pushed me out of my comfort zone. I realized that if I set my mind to it, I could bring technology & fishing together. I went away to college at Indiana Tech to play baseball and study computer science. I chose Indiana because it had a decent program and three rivers to fish right by the school. Go figure fishing had a role to play in one of the biggest decisions of my life. Once I realized that I needed to come back to Chicago, I knew IIT was the best option. I am working on a fishing App right now with another student I met here. I also fish the 31st Harbor every chance I get to sneak away between classes.
